Sample Unit of Work from NESA

Stage 4 Materials and Production Processes

OBJECTIVE: Design and produce a unique low stool for sale in the Taronga Zoo merchandise shop, inspired by the zoo's natural environment and commitment to conservation. The design of this stool is based off the rock patterns that form naturally along the rocky coastlines of Australia, and in particular, Wollongong. The stool is designed to tell a story of our ancestors and how we are shaped (like the coastline) by those before us.
